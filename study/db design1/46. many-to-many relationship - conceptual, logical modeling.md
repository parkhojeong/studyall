* many-to-many relationship - conceptual, logical modeling

* m:n relationship having no relationship attributes example
- conceptual: student(m) - (n)subject
- logical: student(1) - (n)enrollment(n) - (1)subject
- practically, these questions are asked
  - when does a student enroll a subject?(enrollment_application_date)
  - what is the grade of an enrollment?(grade)
  - what enrollment status is a student in?(enrollment_status)

* summary - m:n relationship having relationship attributes
- conceptual 
  - student(1) - (n)enrollment(n) - (1)subject
  - it can be used for oop languages, nosql, rdbms
  - enrollment entity has relationship attributes
