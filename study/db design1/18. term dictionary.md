* why is a term dictionary needed?
- all stakeholders can use consistent terminology
- productivity and system stability boost

* term dictionary based on practice 
- classify by role
- distinguish abbreviation and full english name
- write with detail example how to use term to system

* based on a single word
- use single term of minimum unit
  - ex. 
    - using member_id, product_price in business, define term member, id, product, price
    - so, member_id = member + id, product_price = product + price
- it increase system estimation probability

* cons of using composite term
- term dictionary is fat: all combinations are needed
- hard to keep consistency: if one term is changed, all related terms need to be changed
- flexibility and reusability decreased
conclusion: use single word term

* why business term is included in term dictionary?
- for easier communication between business and developers
- term dictionary is a common language(ubiquitous language) for all stakeholders

* abbreviation unification
- must register in dictionary
