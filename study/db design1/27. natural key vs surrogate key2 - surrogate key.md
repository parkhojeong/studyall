* natural key vs surrogate key2 - surrogate key
- artificial key only for identification
- it is never changed
- system generate or uuid 
- not related to business

* modern db design standard pattern
- pk is an artificial key
  - role: only for identification
  - implementation: system generate or uuid
  - pros: never changed
- apply unique to a natural key
  - role: guarantee uniqueness in business domain
  - implementation: ex. email, login_id
  - pros
    - data integrity: prevent duplication
    - performance benefit: index for unqiue

* summary
- use artificial key for pk, unique for natural keys
