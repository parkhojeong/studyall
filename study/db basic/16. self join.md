* self join
- requirement: show employee name and direct manager name together
+-------------+------------+------------+
| employee_id | name       | manager_id |
+-------------+------------+------------+
| 1           | 김회장     | NULL        |
+-------------+------------+------------+
| 2           | 박사장     | 1           |
+-------------+------------+------------+
| 3           | 이부장     | 2           |
+-------------+------------+------------+
| 4           | 최과장     | 3           |
+-------------+------------+------------+
| 5           | 정대리     | 4           |
+-------------+------------+------------+
| 6           | 홍사원     | 4           |
+-------------+------------+------------+

```sql
select 
    e.name as employee_name,
    manager.name as manager_name
from employees e
left join employees manager on e.manager_id = manager.employee_id
```

* self join concept and basic
- by table alias, employees e join employees managers on e.manager_id = managers.employee_id

* practical tips
- use for hierarchical by self join
  - ex. employee hierarchy, category and subcategory, board and reply board
